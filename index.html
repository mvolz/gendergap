Significant Editor Gender Gap

This is a list of surveys that have been conducted to get at the editor gender gap. Please note that these are not corrected surveys but the raw data. For instance Hill and Shaw 2016 used Pew Surveys to correct the 13% figure at the bottom here to 16%. However, even if these are an underestimate of the total number of registered editors, we still have a significant problem as the most prolific users are more likely to be male. So the percentage of total content produced by women is likely even smaller than the total percentage of male editors. Also please note that these are somewhat geographically biased because most of these surveys are done on English Wikipedia, although there are some global south surveys in this chart as well. Welsh Wikipedia actually has a gender bias in the other direction, 55% of registered editors there are female!
< 1/4 of registered Wikipedia editors are female; active editors may be even lower.
